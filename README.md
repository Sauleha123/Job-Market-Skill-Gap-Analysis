## ğŸ“ˆ Key Insights

* **IT Services and Staffing industries** dominate Data Analyst hiring.
* **Over 84% of job postings target Entry-level candidates**, indicating strong opportunities for freshers.
* **Programming and Soft Skills together account for over 60%** of total skill demand.
* Significant **skill gaps exist between Entry and Senior roles**, especially in:

  * Programming
  * BI Tools
* Senior roles offer **higher average salaries**, though many job titles share similar salary bands, suggesting standardized compensation structures.
* Only **~4% of roles offer Easy Apply**, increasing competition and application effort.
